Resume Tips

Actionable advice to help you get hired faster.

Tailor Your Keywords

Always customize your resume keywords to match the job description. ATS software scans for these exact matches.

Keep it Concise

Recruiters spend an average of 7 seconds on a resume. Keep yours to 1-2 pages maximum.

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers to show impact. "Increased sales by 20%" is better than "Responsible for sales".

Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Spearheaded", "Developed", or "Optimized".

Avoid Personal Info

Do not include age, marital status, or full address. It's outdated and a security risk.
