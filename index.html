Pro Tips

Expert Resume Tips

Boost your chances with these proven strategies from hiring experts.

1

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Led", "Developed", "Achieved" to make your accomplishments stand out.

2

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and percentages to demonstrate impact: "Increased sales by 35%" is more powerful than "Improved sales".

3

Keep It Concise

Aim for 1-2 pages max. Recruiters spend only 6 seconds on initial review, so every word must count.

4

Match Keywords

Mirror keywords from the job description. ATS systems scan for specific terms related to the role.

5

Use Simple Formatting

Avoid tables, graphics, and fancy fonts. ATS systems work best with clean, standard formatting.
